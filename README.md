# Chatbase
# What is Chatbase?

> Build AI agents trained on your own business knowledge.

---

## Overview

Chatbase is an AI agent platform that enables businesses to create intelligent AI assistants trained on their own data. Instead of relying on predefined rules or keyword matching, Chatbase uses modern Large Language Models (LLMs) to understand natural language and answer questions based on your content.

Businesses can train AI agents using:

- Websites
- Documentation
- PDFs
- FAQs
- Knowledge Bases
- Notion
- Uploaded Files

The result is an AI assistant capable of providing fast, accurate, and contextual responses 24 hours a day.

---

# Why Chatbase?

Modern businesses need faster customer support.

Traditional support systems often involve:

- Long waiting times
- Large support teams
- Repetitive questions
- High operational costs

Chatbase helps automate these repetitive conversations while allowing human agents to focus on more complex issues.
